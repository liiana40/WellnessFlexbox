Exercise

Exercise is a body activity that enhances or maintains physical fitness and overall health and wellness. It is a very important to start your day with peace, enjoyment, and exercise.

Yoga

Slow movements and deep breathing increase blood flow and warm up muscles, while holding a pose can build strength.

Workout

Walking

Walking for 30 minutes a day or more on most days of the week is a great way to improve or maintain your overall health.
